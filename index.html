About Us

Coco International - Since 1997

Coco international is a well-known Sri lankan supplier of Coconut fiber Products for last 20 years. We put up our first Coconut coir factory at Bandirippuwa, Lunuwila, Srilanka to extract Coir fiber the plant is equipped with machines to process the husk into fiber.

Coco international has a enviable Capacity of supplying variety of Process Coconut coir Products. The products meet the international quality to treat our Clients in a Successive manner.
